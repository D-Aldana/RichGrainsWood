About Me

Who am I?

Originally from Stony Plain, Alberta, I studied carpentry at Vancouver Island University in Nanaimo, British Columbia. In 2017, I founded Rich Grains Wood as an Instagram page, and after moving to Victoria in 2018, things started picking up. I have since relocated to Kamloops where I do work in BC's interior. I’ve had the experience of working with many unique and different species of wood. Being able to provide clients with some amazing custom pieces! Rich Grains Wood, originally started out on the side with the intent of growing into the true passion of wood working and carpentry! With that route I was able to learn so much of the trade and meet some great people along the way, and that’s the best part! Being able to share knowledge, ideas, and be apart of an amazing wood working community.

Coffee tables, dining tables, cutting boards, charcuterie boards, and many more! I am very happy to discuss and create whatever your Imagination presents.

Behind the Logo

The logo represents where I am from, to where Rich Grains Wood was created, and everything in between. Keeping all aspects of growing up with me! From High school carpentry class in Stony Plain to watching my Grandpa do small bits of woodworking in Vernon when I was very young. From helping out on the farm in Saskatchewan to becoming a carpenter in Nanaimo. The move to Victoria where RGW really kicked off and now Kamloops! So many places and people have influenced my wood working journey, and I’m looking forward to keeping it going!
